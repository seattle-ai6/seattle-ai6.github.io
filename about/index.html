About Seattle AI6

Are you ready to Kickass in AI? So are we, so when we saw that nurture.ai was rolling out their AI Saturdays Syllabus nationwide, we couldn't wait to get started!

Our first chapter meeting was on the last Saturday of December 2017, in a downtown Seattle Starbucks. A dozen or so AI enthusiasts gathered to find out what we were about.

Over the next few weeks, committed AI programmers stuck with us while we found a venue sponsor for our first 15-week session. Thankfully, Code Fellows opened their doors to us, and we kicked off 2018 digging into AI, using fast.ai and Stanford study materials to guide our study sessions.
